Biography

Mushtaq Masih is the founder of Heavenly Hand Ministry and the Heavenly Light School. With a heart dedicated to serving underprivileged children and families, he has devoted his life to Christian ministry, education, and community support.

He established a school to provide free education for children from brick-kiln and low-income communities, ensuring they receive academic learning along with spiritual formation and Christian values.

His ongoing vision includes expanding the school, building a community kitchen, supporting orphans, and establishing a medical support unit to serve the poor with dignity.

Journey & Milestones

  • Repentance and full dedication of retirement to ministry work.
  • Started house fellowship and planted a small local church.
  • Founded Heavenly Light School with an initial batch of 47 children.
  • Launched outreach visits, school-meal plans and vision for orphan care.
  • Praying and planning for a community kitchen and basic medical support unit.

Heavenly Light School

The school offers basic literacy, moral teaching, discipline and a safe Christian environment for children from brick-kiln and low-income families.
