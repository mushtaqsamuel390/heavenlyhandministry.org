Biography

Mushtaq masih is the founder of Heavenly Hand Ministry and the Heavenly Light School. With a heart dedicated to serving underprivileged children and families, he has devoted his life to Christian ministry, education, and community support. His vision began after surrendering his life to God and receiving a call to serve the poor, the needy, and the forgotten.

He established a school to provide free education for children from brick-kiln and low-income communities, ensuring they receive academic learning along with spiritual formation and Christian values. Through the ministry, he continues outreach missions, prayer meetings, family counseling, and community development work.

mushtaq masih believes that transformation begins with love, compassion, and the Gospel of Christ. His ongoing vision includes expanding the school, building a community kitchen, supporting orphans, and establishing a hospital to serve the poor with dignity. Every step forward in this mission is a testimony of God’s faithfulness.

Journey & Milestones

  • Repentance and full dedication of retirement to ministry work.
  • Started house fellowship and planted a small local church.
  • Founded Heavenly Light School with an initial batch of 47 children.
  • Launched outreach visits, school-meal plans and vision for orphan care.
  • Praying and planning for a community kitchen and basic medical support unit.

Heavenly Light School

The school offers basic literacy, moral teaching, discipline and a safe Christian environment for children from brick-kiln and low-income families.

Key Projects

01. Daily School Operations

Teacher support, rent, electricity, books, copies and basic uniforms for the students.

02. Orphan Care Program

Targeted support for orphan and semi-orphan children — school fees, notebooks, meals and regular pastoral care.

03. Community Kitchen (Vision)

A simple kitchen to provide hot meals to school children and poorest families at least once or twice a week.

04. Basic Health & Medical Help

Connecting families to basic checkups, medicines and emergency help through local Christian doctors and clinics.
